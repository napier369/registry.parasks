Sources policy

  • Prefer primary sources (vendor docs, standards bodies, official publications).
  • Secondary sources (Wikipedia, blogs) are allowed only as provenance inputs, not as authoritative definitions.
  • Never copy copyrighted text; link to sources and derive facts.
  • Every wiki topic page must include a Sources section.
  • Canonical meaning lives in /data; wiki pages are narrative and must not redefine identifiers.
