Public use & citation

Records in registry.parasks are public identifiers and may be cited, linked, and referenced without permission, provided the canonical identifier is used.

How to cite

  • Use the permanent identifier: https://registry.parasks/id/<ID>
  • Do not cite /page URLs when referring to identity
  • For machine use, prefer /data/<ID>.jsonld

Example

Authority. registry.parasks.
https://registry.parasks/id/E000001

Stability

  • Identifiers are permanent.
  • Records are append-only.
  • History is auditable.
