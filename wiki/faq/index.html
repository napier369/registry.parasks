Frequently Asked Questions

What is registry.parasks?

A sovereign identifier registry that issues permanent, auditable identifiers for entities, agents, documents, and events.

Is this a wiki like Wikipedia?

No. The wiki here is documentation only. Identity lives under /id, machine truth under /data.

Can identifiers change or be deleted?

No. Identifiers are permanent. Changes are recorded as new records and events.

How should I cite a record?

Use the canonical identifier URL, for example: https://registry.parasks/id/E000001.

Who controls the registry?

Authority and registrar roles are explicitly identified and published in the registry.
