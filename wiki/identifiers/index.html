Identifiers

Identifiers in registry.parasks are permanent, opaque, and issued by authority. Meaning is carried in records, not encoded in the identifier string.

Form

  • Pattern: /id/<PREFIX><NUMBER>/
  • Machine record: /data/<ID>.jsonld
  • Human page: /page/<ID>/

Prefixes

  • E — Entity (real-world entity or concept)
  • A — Actor/Agent (issuer / registrar)
  • D — Document (governing / normative document)
  • X — Event (issuance / governance event)

Rules

  • Never delete or reuse identifiers.
  • Canonical truth is the machine record under /data.
  • /id/<ID>/ redirects to the human page /page/<ID>/.
  • Wiki pages explain; they do not redefine identifiers.
  • Every issuance/change is recorded in /audit/logs/.

Examples

  • Authority: E000001
  • Registrar: A000001
  • Genesis event: X000001
  • Operating charter: D000001
  • Entity example: E000004 (IBM)
