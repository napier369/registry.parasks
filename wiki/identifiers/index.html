Identifiers

Identifiers in registry.parasks are permanent, opaque, and issued by authority. Meaning is carried in records, not encoded in the identifier string.

Form

  • Pattern: /id/<PREFIX><NUMBER>
  • Examples: E000001, A000001, D000001, X000002

Prefixes

  • E — Entity
  • A — Agent
  • D — Document
  • X — Event

Rules

  • Identifiers are issued, never derived.
  • Identifiers are never reused or deleted.
  • Changes create new records and audit entries.

Resolution

  • /id resolves to /page for humans.
  • Machine-readable form is available under /data.
