Audit & Provenance

All changes in registry.parasks are append-only and recorded. Identity is permanent; history is explicit.

Audit log

  • Location: /audit/logs/
  • Format: sequential JSON files (000001.json, 000002.json, …)
  • Pointer: audit/logs/index.json indicates the latest entry

What gets audited

  • Issuance of any new identifier
  • Recording of events
  • Formal status declarations (e.g., v1 freeze)

Provenance

  • Actors are identified (e.g., Registrar)
  • Events reference affected records
  • Timestamps are recorded at issuance
