Authority

registry.parasks operates as a sovereign identifier registry. Authority is explicit, issued, and recorded; it is not inferred, delegated by software vendors, or derived from external platforms. [1][2]

Source of authority

  • Issuing authority: E000001
  • Registrar: A000001
  • Genesis event: X000001

Principles

  • Identifiers are issued, never reused, never deleted.
  • Identity is permanent; representation may change.
  • All changes are additive and recorded in the audit log. [2]

What this is not

  • Not a wiki that creates identity by editing text.
  • Not dependent on IBM, Wikimedia, or third-party namespaces. [3]
  • Not mutable without record.
