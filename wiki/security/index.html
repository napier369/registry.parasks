Security & trust

This registry separates identity from representation and records history in an append-only audit log. Trust is established by published authority records and, optionally, by cryptographic signing of manifests and snapshots.

Current trust anchors

  • Authority: E000001
  • Registrar: A000001
  • Audit log: audit/logs/index.json
  • Key endpoint: .well-known/registry-keys.json

Integrity model

  • Identifiers are permanent and never reused.
  • Records are published as JSON-LD under /data.
  • Every issuance is recorded in /audit/logs.

Signing (optional)

  • Publish verification keys under /.well-known/registry-keys.json.
  • Sign snapshot manifests (future hardening).
  • Keep old keys so historical manifests remain verifiable.
